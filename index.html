Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2025

Overview

This application uses Facebook Login for Business to allow authorized users to access and display public content (e.g., posts, images) from Facebook Pages they manage. No private or personally identifiable information is collected, stored, or shared.

Data Usage

The only data accessed includes publicly available content from Pages administered by the user. This data is used solely for display within our system and is not processed for advertising, analytics, or any third-party purposes.

Data Deletion

Users may revoke access at any time through their Facebook account settings. Upon revocation or upon request, any minimal identifiers (e.g., Page ID, user ID) are deleted immediately.
